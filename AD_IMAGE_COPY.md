## B) PRIMARY TEXT (3 Variations)

### Primary Text 1 -- SHORT (Hook + Clarity)

There's a difference between using AI and building with AI.

Most developers are on the wrong side of that line. 120-minute live workshop. A real app gets built and deployed. No slides. Rs 4,999 value, currently free. 5,000+ already registered.

### Primary Text 2 -- MEDIUM (Problem, Insight, Belief Shift)

You open Cursor. You autocomplete. You ship code you half-understand.

When it breaks in production, you paste the error back into ChatGPT and hope for the best. You've been doing this for 8 months now. You've watched 40+ videos about AI agents. You've bookmarked 15 repos. You've finished zero projects.

The problem was never information. It was that nobody showed you how to go from prompt to production in one sitting.

This workshop builds and deploys a real AI-powered app in 120 minutes. Live. Not a recording. Not a pitch. You watch the code ship. Rs 4,999 value, free for a limited time. 5,000+ engineers already in.

### Primary Text 3 -- LONG (Story-Driven)

Last Tuesday, a senior developer at a Bengaluru startup told me something that stuck.

He said: "I use Cursor every day. I prompt ChatGPT maybe 30 times a day. But if you asked me to build an AI agent from scratch, I wouldn't know where to start. My junior colleague, 2 years out of college, built one in a weekend. I didn't even understand her pull request."

He's not alone. 49% of Indian developers now say they fear AI will make their role irrelevant within 5 years. Not because AI is smarter than them. Because someone who builds with AI will always outperform someone who only uses it.

The gap isn't knowledge. It's shipping. He had watched more AI content than most people. He'd started 3 courses. Finished none. Bookmarked dozens of tools. Used none seriously. Months of consuming, zero deploying.

That gap doesn't close with another YouTube playlist. It closes the first time you actually build something and see it work.

That's what this workshop is. 120 minutes. Live. One real AI-powered app goes from idea to deployed while you watch. You'll see the exact tools, the exact process, the exact moment it goes live. No slides. No theory. No pitch.

Rs 4,999 workshop, free for a limited time. 5,000+ engineers have already registered. The people who build aren't waiting for permission. They're already moving.

## META / ANDROMEDA ALIGNMENT NOTES

- All hooks trigger within first 3 seconds (image text is the hook)
- Language is conversational, not promotional
- No tricks, no fake scarcity -- numbers are real (5,000+ registrations, Rs 4,999 anchor, 56% salary data, 49% fear stat)
- Primary texts escalate in depth: short for retargeting, medium for warm, long for cold
- Headlines are clear and benefit-oriented, no clickbait
- All ads encourage meaningful stops (self-identification, curiosity, social proof) rather than impulse clicks
- Suitable for CBO / ASC testing with 3-5 creatives per ad set
